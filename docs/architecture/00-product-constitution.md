# 00 - Product Constitution

## Abbot Study Product Constitution

### Purpose

Abbot Study exists to help learners achieve deep understanding through structured, sequential, mastery-based learning enhanced by trustworthy artificial intelligence.

The platform is designed as an Educational Operating System rather than a simple AI tutoring application.

---

# Product Vision

Build the world's most trusted AI-powered learning platform that combines rigorous educational principles with modern artificial intelligence while keeping humans in control of official learning content.

---

# Educational Philosophy

The platform shall always prioritize:

1. Understanding over memorization.
2. Mastery over completion.
3. Sequential learning over random exploration.
4. Source-grounded teaching over hallucinated explanations.
5. Human-approved curriculum over automatically generated curriculum.
6. Long-term retention over short-term performance.

---

# Core User Roles

## Learner

Consumes approved learning content.

May:

* Learn
* Ask questions
* Complete assessments
* Teach Ariel
* Review progress

May not:

* Modify official curriculum
* Reorder learning pathways

---

## Institution Administrator

Manages institutional configuration.

May:

* Manage institutions
* Manage users
* Manage permissions
* Configure institutional settings

---

## Academic Administrator

Owns curriculum quality.

May:

* Review imported content
* Approve curriculum
* Approve lessons
* Manage assessments
* Publish official learning content

---

## Platform Administrator

Owns the technical platform.

May:

* Manage infrastructure
* Monitor events
* Audit activity
* Configure system-wide behavior

---

The learning engine may enrich this journey but may never violate its ordering rules.

---

# Non-Negotiable Invariants

The following rules are permanent architectural constraints.

## Sequential Learning

Learners unlock content sequentially.

No capability may bypass official progression rules.

---

## Mastery

Progression requires mastery.

Completion alone is never sufficient.

---

## Curriculum Authority

Only approved curriculum becomes official learning material.

Imported or AI-generated content remains provisional until approved.

---

## Explainability

Every AI-generated explanation should be traceable to approved academic content whenever possible.

---

## Human Oversight

Administrative users retain authority over:

* Curriculum
* Lessons
* Assessments
* Quality decisions

---

# AI Authority Boundaries

Artificial intelligence may:

* Explain
* Teach
* Clarify
* Recommend
* Assess
* Generate supporting material
* Personalize explanations

Artificial intelligence may not:

* Change official curriculum
* Reorder learning progression
* Publish curriculum automatically
* Override mastery rules
* Bypass approval workflows

---

# Data Ownership

Learners own:

* Personal progress
* Learning history
* Preferences

Institutions own:

* Institutional configuration
* Administrative policies

The platform owns:

* System operation
* Infrastructure
* Event processing

Official curriculum is governed through approved administrative workflows.

---

# Administrative Authority

Administrative actions must be:

* Auditable
* Traceable
* Reviewable

Critical educational decisions require explicit human action.

---

# Architectural Principle

Education drives AI.

AI never drives education.

Whenever these principles conflict, educational integrity takes precedence.
